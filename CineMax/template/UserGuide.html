User Guide

Get help and learn how to use our OTT platform effectively.

Getting Started

  • Set up your account and start streaming
  • Navigate the platform's features and sections
  • Customize your profile and preferences

Searching and Browsing

  • Use the search function to find specific titles
  • Browse by categories, genres, and collections
  • Discover new content based on your interests

Watchlist and Collections

  • Add shows and movies to your Watchlist for later
  • Create and manage your own custom collections
  • Share your Watchlist and collections with friends

Streaming and Playback

  • Start streaming on any compatible device
  • Use playback controls like pause, rewind, and fast-forward
  • Adjust video quality based on your internet connection

Profiles and Parental Controls

  • Set up individual profiles for family members
  • Enable parental controls to restrict access to mature content
  • Monitor and manage your children's viewing activity

Offline Viewing

  • Download content to watch later without an internet connection
  • Available for select titles based on licensing agreements
  • Manage your downloaded content in the "Downloads" section

Live Streaming

  • Access live events and shows in real-time
  • Participate in live chats and interact with other viewers
  • Set reminders for upcoming live streams

Troubleshooting

  • Resolve common issues like buffering or playback errors
  • Check your internet connection and device compatibility
  • Contact our support team for further assistance
